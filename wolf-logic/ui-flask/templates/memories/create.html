About Memories

Memories are pieces of information that OpenMemory stores and makes retrievable for your applications. When you create a memory:

  • The content is embedded using vector embeddings for semantic search
  • Categories are automatically generated using AI to organize your memories
  • The memory becomes immediately searchable and accessible to your apps
  • You can pause, archive, or delete memories at any time
